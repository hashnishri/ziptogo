Support - ZipOnGo

Welcome to the ZipOnGo Support Center. We're here to help you get the best out of our app.

Common Questions

  • How do I unzip a file? — Tap and hold on any ZIP file, then select “Unzip” from the options.
  • How do I create a new ZIP file? — Select multiple files and tap “Zip” from the toolbar.
  • Can I open password-protected archives? — Yes, simply enter the password when prompted.
