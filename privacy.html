1) What we collect and why

A. From Apple (to fulfill purchases)

Purchase & subscription info (StoreKit): product IDs, transaction status/receipts, region, and dates.

Purpose: App functionality (deliver purchases, restore entitlements, customer support), fraud prevention, and accounting.

B. From your device (app operation)

  • Diagnostics & crash data (Firebase Crashlytics): crash reports, stack traces, device model, OS version, app version, timestamps. Purpose: reliability, debugging, safety, and quality.
  • App usage & performance (Firebase Analytics): screens opened, feature usage counts, session metrics. Purpose: aggregate analytics to improve features and UX.

We do not collect precise GPS location, contacts, photos, microphone input, or the contents of your documents. Your files are processed on device unless you explicitly share or export them.

C. For advertising (only if you consent to tracking)

Ad identifiers and events: Apple’s IDFA (if allowed) and ad interaction/impression data via Google Mobile Ads (AdMob).

Purpose: deliver and measure ads; prevent fraud. See ATT & Tracking.

2) App Store “App Privacy” categories

  • Identifiers: IDFA (if you allow), limited device identifiers (for diagnostics/anti-abuse).
  • Purchases: in-app purchase / subscription metadata (no payment card data).
  • Usage Data: app interactions and performance metrics.
  • Diagnostics: crash logs and stability signals.

Linking: Purchases and diagnostics can be linked to your device/app instance to provide functionality and safety.

Tracking: Only advertising identifiers are used for tracking if you tap Allow on the ATT prompt.

3) Apple App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

ZipOnGo asks for permission before using data to track you across other companies’ apps and websites. If you decline, you will still see ads, but they may be less relevant. You can change permission anytime in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

4) Third-party processors (SDKs)

We use trusted processors acting on our behalf under data-processing agreements:

  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) — ad delivery, measurement, and fraud prevention. Data: identifiers (IDFA if allowed), coarse device info, ad events.
  • Firebase Crashlytics — crash reports and diagnostics.
  • Firebase Analytics — aggregate usage analytics; no precise location or contacts.

Each provider processes data under its own policy and retention. We configure them to the minimum necessary for the stated purposes.

5) Children

ZipOnGo is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child used the app and provided data, contact us to delete it.

6) Data retention

  • Crash & diagnostics: up to 24 months (provider defaults) or until we complete a verified deletion request.
  • Purchase records: retained as required by law and accounting (typically up to 7 years).
  • Advertising data: per AdMob’s standard retention schedules.

We keep data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Policy.

7) Security

We use industry-standard safeguards (encryption in transit, access controls). No method is 100% secure, but we continuously improve our protections.

8) Your choices & legal rights

In-app / device controls

  • Tracking (ATT): allow/deny in iOS Settings.
  • Reset IDFA: iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → Reset Advertising Identifier.
  • Crash/analytics at OS level: you can disable device analytics sharing in iOS Settings.

EEA/UK, California, and similar jurisdictions

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, port, restrict, or object to processing, and to opt-out of “sharing” for cross-context behavioral advertising (California).

To exercise rights: email us at hashni.work@gmail.com. We will verify your request and respond within applicable timeframes.

9) International transfers

If data is transferred outside your region, we use lawful mechanisms (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses) and appropriate safeguards.

10) No sale, limited sharing

We do not sell personal data. We share data only with processors listed above, service providers under contract, when required by law, or during a business transfer (merger/acquisition) with equivalent protections.

11) Changes

We may update this Policy. We will update the “Effective date” and, where required, provide notice in the app or by other means.
