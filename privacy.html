Privacy Policy - ZipOnGo

At ZipOnGo, we value your privacy. This document describes how we handle your data and ensure your information stays secure.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal files or documents. Limited app analytics may be collected to improve app performance and stability.

2. Data Storage

All files you manage with ZipOnGo stay on your device or your connected cloud storage accounts (e.g., iCloud, Dropbox). We never upload or read your files without your explicit action.

3. Ads & Analytics

We use Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) for in-app advertising and Firebase Analytics to understand app usage trends. These services may collect anonymized device and usage data.

4. In-App Purchases

Payments are processed securely through Apple’s App Store. We do not store any billing or payment details.

5. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, contact us at privacy@hashni.com.
