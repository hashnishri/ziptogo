1) What we collect and why

A. From Apple (to fulfill purchases)

Purchase & subscription info (StoreKit): product IDs, transaction receipts/status, region, dates.

Purpose: deliver purchases, restore entitlements, fraud prevention, and accounting.

B. From your device (app operation)

  • Diagnostics & crash data (e.g., Firebase Crashlytics): crash logs, stack traces, device/OS/app version, timestamps. Purpose: reliability, debugging, quality.
  • Usage & performance (e.g., Firebase Analytics): screen views and feature counts in aggregate. Purpose: improve features and UX.

C. For advertising (only if you consent to tracking)

Ad identifiers/events: Apple’s IDFA (if allowed) and ad interactions via Google AdMob. Purpose: deliver/measure ads; prevent fraud. See ATT.

2) What we do not collect

We do not collect precise GPS location, contacts, microphone input, photo contents, or the contents of your files. File operations occur on device or within your chosen cloud provider; nothing is uploaded to our servers unless you explicitly share/export it.

3) Cloud connections

You may connect third-party cloud services (Dropbox, Google Drive, Box, OneDrive). Authentication and file access are handled securely by each provider’s SDK. Hashni Inc does not receive your cloud credentials or files. Your use of those services is governed by their own terms and privacy policies.

4) App Store “App Privacy” categories

  • Identifiers: IDFA (if allowed) and limited device identifiers.
  • Purchases: in-app purchase/subscription metadata.
  • Usage Data: app interactions and performance metrics.
  • Diagnostics: crash logs and stability signals.

Linking: Purchases and diagnostics may be linked to your device/app instance to provide functionality and safety.

Tracking: Only ad identifiers are used for tracking and only if you tap Allow on the ATT prompt.

5) Apple App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

ZipOnGo asks for permission before using data to track you across apps and websites owned by other companies. If you decline, you may still see ads, but they may be less relevant. Change permission anytime in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

6) Third-party processors (SDKs)

We use trusted processors acting on our behalf under data-processing agreements:

  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) — ad delivery/measurement and fraud prevention. Data: identifiers (IDFA if allowed), coarse device info, ad events. Policy
  • Firebase Crashlytics — crash reports and diagnostics. Policy
  • Firebase Analytics — aggregate usage analytics. Policy

We configure these services to the minimum necessary for the purposes described above.

7) Children

ZipOnGo is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8) Data retention

  • Crash/diagnostics: up to 24 months (provider defaults) or until deletion upon verified request.
  • Purchase records: retained as required by law/accounting (typically up to 7 years).
  • Advertising data: per AdMob’s standard retention schedules.

9) Security

We use industry-standard safeguards (encryption in transit, access controls). No method is 100% secure, but we continuously improve protections.

10) Your choices & legal rights

In-app / device controls

  • Tracking (ATT): allow/deny in iOS Settings.
  • Reset IDFA: iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → Reset Advertising Identifier.
  • iOS Analytics: you can disable device analytics sharing in iOS Settings.

Regional rights (EEA/UK, California, etc.)

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, port, restrict, or object to processing, and to opt-out of “sharing” for cross-context behavioral advertising.

To exercise rights: email hashni.work@gmail.com. We will verify and respond within applicable timeframes.

11) International transfers

If data is transferred outside your region, we use lawful mechanisms (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses) and appropriate safeguards.

12) No sale, limited sharing

We do not sell personal data. We share data only with processors listed above, service providers under contract, when required by law, or during a business transfer with equivalent protections.

13) Changes

We may update this Policy. We will update the “Effective date” and, where required, provide notice in the app or by other means.
